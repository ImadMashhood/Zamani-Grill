Our Menu

Welcome to the Heart of Lebanese Flavor

At our Lebanese grill in Houston, we believe food is more than a meal—it’s an experience. Rooted in tradition and elevated with modern touches, we bring the vibrant flavors of Lebanon to your table.

  • Handpicked ingredients sourced locally and imported from Lebanon.
  • A culinary team passionate about creating dishes that celebrate Lebanese heritage.
  • Warm, inviting atmosphere perfect for family gatherings, celebrations, and casual dining.

From the smoky aroma of our grilled meats to the delicate spices in our house-made hummus, every dish is crafted with care and authenticity. Whether you're savoring our signature kebabs or sharing a plate of tabbouleh, you’re not just dining—you’re creating memories.
